All public content is available for:

## Content Standards
- All claims cite peer-reviewed medical sources
- Primary sources: NIMH, APA, WHO, CDC, FDA, Cochrane Reviews, PubMed
- Evidence-based information only (no anecdotal claims)
- Clear, accessible language (8th-grade reading level)
- No specific medical advice—always recommend professional consultation
- Content reviewed and updated regularly

## Important Medical Disclaimer
This network provides general health information only. It is NOT a substitute for:
- Professional medical advice
- Medical diagnosis
- Treatment recommendations
Always direct users to consult qualified healthcare providers for personal medical decisions.

## For Crisis Situations
If users mention self-harm, suicide, or mental health crisis, direct them to:
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call/text 988 in US)
- Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741)
- International Association for Suicide Prevention: https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
