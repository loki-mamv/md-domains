Risks & Mitigation

Google E-E-A-T Penalties ‚Äî Health content under intense scrutiny. New domains start with zero authority.

Mitigation: Every article cites peer-reviewed literature. Named authors with credentials. "Medically reviewed" badges. MedicalWebPage schema markup. Build authority gradually ‚Äî quality over volume.

Moldova TLD Risk ‚Äî .md is Moldova's country code. Registry policy changes could affect domains.

Mitigation: Moldova has had stable TLD policies for 15+ years. nic.md is ICANN-accredited. Multi-year registrations available. Multi-year registrations lock in pricing. Worst case: 301 redirect to .com backups.

FTC / FDA Compliance ‚Äî Pharma-adjacent content has advertising and claims regulations.

Mitigation: Clear medical disclaimers on every page. FTC-compliant affiliate disclosures. No unapproved drug claims. Educational content only ‚Äî never prescriptive.

Competition / Copycats ‚Äî Others notice the .md health domain play.

Mitigation: First-mover advantage. Premium domains already registered. 25-site network effect is extremely hard to replicate. Content + backlinks compound over time.
