Awell Health Interview Prep

Meeting with: Thomas Vande Casteele (Co-founder & CEO)

Company Overview

  • What they do: Care orchestration platform for clinicians — digital care pathways, workflow automation, EMR integration
  • Founded: 2016 in Brussels, Belgium
  • Funding: $7.4M raised (Seed rounds) from Octopus Ventures, LocalGlobe
  • Team size: ~25 people
  • Competitors: TigerConnect, Fabric, Ferrum Health

About Thomas

  • Co-founder & CEO since 2016
  • Background: 8 years in e-commerce/digital marketing (Samsung, Audi, Nestlé)
  • Also writes for CareOps (healthcare ops thought leadership)

What Awell Does

  • Care pathway designer (drag-and-drop clinical workflows)
  • Patient engagement tools
  • EMR integration layer
  • Content management for clinical protocols
  • Activity feeds and data export

Questions to Ask Thomas

  1. "What's the biggest bottleneck you're seeing in care orchestration adoption?"

  3. "What does your ideal customer profile look like today vs. where you want it to be?"
  4. "What's the one thing that would 10x Awell's growth right now?"
  5. "How are you thinking about the US market vs. Europe?"
  6. "What made you transition from e-commerce/marketing to healthcare?"
  7. "How do you differentiate from point solutions in the market?"
